Giovanni Lorenzo Bernini

Giovanni Lorenzo Bernini (Gian Lorenzo Bernini; Italian. Giovanni Lorenzo Bernini; December 7, 1598, Naples - November 28, 1680, Rome) - Italian architect and sculptor. He was a prominent architect and leading sculptor of his time, considered the creator of the Baroque style of sculpture. As noted, "What Shakespeare is for drama, so Bernini became for sculpture: the first pan-European sculptor, whose name is instantly identified with a certain manner and vision, and whose influence was unreasonably strong ..."
