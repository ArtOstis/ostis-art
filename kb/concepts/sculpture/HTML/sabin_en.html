The Rape of the Sabine Women

“The Rape of the Sabine Women” (Italian. Ratto delle sabine) is a marble sculpture of Florentine sculptor Giambologni, a classic example of the sculpture of Mannerism, a recognized masterpiece of this master [1]. It is conditionally assumed that the composition depicts a scene from the Roman legend about the abduction of Sabine women by the Romans (the so-called "Sabine War").
