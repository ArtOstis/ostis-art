The Abduction of Proserpina

The Abduction of Proserpina (Italian. Ratto di Proserpina), or Pluto and Proserpina, a marble sculpture by Giovanni Bernini, was created in 1621-1622, when the sculptor was 23 years old. Located in the Borghese Gallery (Rome). The height of the statue is 295 cm. The pupil of Bernini, Francois Girardon, in 1699 created the sculptural group Abduction of Proserpina. “I defeated marble and made it plastic, like wax ...” wrote Giovanni Lorenzo Bernini.
