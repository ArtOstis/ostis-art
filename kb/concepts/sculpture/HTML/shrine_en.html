Tomb of antipope John XXIII

The tomb of antipope John XXIII is a bronze and marble tombstone created by Donatello and Michelozzo and located in the baptistery of the Cathedral of Santa Maria del Fiore (Duomo), where the antipope John XXIII rests (Balthasar Cossa, circa 1360-1419). Its construction was ordered by executors of John shortly after his death on December 22, 1419 and completed in the 1420s
